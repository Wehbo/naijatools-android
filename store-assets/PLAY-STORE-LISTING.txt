NaijaTools is Nigeria's most complete free digital toolkit — designed by Nigerians, for Nigerians.

Everything runs on your device. Your files never leave your phone.

━━━ 📚 STUDENT HUB ━━━

🎓 GPA Calculator
Calculate your semester GPA on the Nigerian 5-point grading scale. Add all your courses, units and grades — get your result instantly.

🎓 CGPA Calculator
Track your Cumulative GPA across all semesters. Know your exact degree classification at any point.

📝 JAMB Score Predictor
Enter your UTME subject scores to calculate your total and see your admission outlook for Federal Universities, State Universities, and Polytechnics.

📊 Post-UTME Aggregate Calculator
Calculate your weighted admission aggregate from your JAMB and Post-UTME scores. Choose your institution's weighting formula.

📋 O-Level Points Calculator
Convert your WAEC/NECO grades to O-Level aggregate points for Direct Entry admission.

━━━ 📄 PDF TOOLS ━━━

🔗 Merge PDF — Combine multiple PDF files into one document
✂️ Split PDF — Extract specific pages from any PDF
🗜️ Compress PDF — Reduce file size while keeping quality
🔄 Rotate PDF — Fix sideways or upside-down PDF pages

━━━ 🖼️ IMAGE TOOLS ━━━

📷 Image Compressor — Reduce image file size with quality control slider
📐 Image Resizer — Resize to exact pixel dimensions with aspect ratio lock
✂️ Image Cropper — Crop to 1:1, 16:9, 4:3, 9:16 or free dimensions
🔄 JPG to PNG — Convert JPEG images to PNG format instantly

━━━ 💼 BUSINESS & UTILITY ━━━

🧾 Invoice Generator — Create professional Nigerian invoices with VAT calculation and bank payment details. Download and share instantly.

💱 Naira Converter — Convert between NGN, USD, GBP, and EUR using NAFEX and parallel market rates.

🏦 Loan Calculator — Calculate monthly repayments, total interest, and see amortization for any Nigerian loan.

📱 QR Code Generator — Create custom QR codes for URLs, WhatsApp links, payments, or any text. Customize colors and download as PNG.

📏 Unit Converter — Convert length, weight, temperature, area, volume, and speed between any units.

🏢 Business Name Generator — Generate creative Nigerian business names by industry and style (Modern, Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Professional).

### BEFORE BUILDING
[ ] Register at play.google.com/console ($25 one-time fee)
[ ] Create new app in Play Console
[ ] Fill in app name: "NaijaTools - Digital Toolkit"

### KEYSTORE (do this ONCE — never lose these files)
[ ] Generate keystore on any PC:
    keytool -genkey -v -keystore naijatools.jks \
      -alias naijatools -keyalg RSA \
      -keysize 2048 -validity 10000
[ ] Back up naijatools.jks in a safe place (Google Drive, USB)
[ ] Convert to base64:
    base64 -w 0 naijatools.jks > keystore-b64.txt  (Linux/Mac)
    certutil -encode naijatools.jks keystore-b64.txt  (Windows)

### GITHUB SECRETS (repo → Settings → Secrets → Actions)
[ ] KEYSTORE_BASE64  — paste contents of keystore-b64.txt
[ ] KEYSTORE_PASSWORD — your keystore password
[ ] KEY_ALIAS — naijatools
[ ] KEY_PASSWORD — your key password

### BUILD THE AAB
[ ] Push any commit to trigger GitHub Actions
[ ] Download artifact: NaijaTools-release-aab-N.zip
[ ] Extract to get: app-release.aab

### PRIVACY POLICY
[ ] Go to your GitHub repo → Settings → Pages → Enable GitHub Pages
[ ] Upload privacy-policy.html to repo root
[ ] Your URL: https://[username].github.io/[repo-name]/privacy-policy.html
[ ] Paste this URL into Play Console → App Content → Privacy Policy

### STORE LISTING ASSETS (upload from store-assets/ folder)
[ ] App icon: icon-512.png (512×512 PNG) ✅ included
[ ] Feature graphic: feature-graphic-1024x500.png (1024×500) ✅ included
[ ] Phone screenshots: screenshot-phone-1.png, -2.png, -3.png ✅ included
    (minimum 2 required — we have 3)

### UPLOAD AAB & SUBMIT
[ ] Production → Create new release → Upload app-release.aab

## TIPS FOR FASTER APPROVAL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. The app loads local files (file:///android_asset) not a remote URL
   → This avoids the "WebView wrapper" policy rejection

2. We have real native functionality (file picker, share, offline)
   → Google requires WebView apps to add "native" value beyond a website

3. Data safety section: declare NO data collection
   → Google will verify this — our app genuinely collects nothing

4. If rejected for any reason, check the rejection email carefully
   and resubmit after fixing — most first-time rejections are fixable
