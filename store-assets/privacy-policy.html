NaijaTools Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Last updated: June 2025

Effective Date: June 1, 2025
Short version: NaijaTools processes everything locally on your device. We do not collect, store, upload, or sell any of your personal data or files. Your documents and images never leave your phone.

1. Who We Are

NaijaTools ("we", "us", "our") is a free digital toolkit application designed for Nigerian users, providing tools for document processing, academic calculations, currency conversion, business utilities, and more.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear: NaijaTools does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Files you upload or process (PDFs, images, documents)
  • Your location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioural tracking data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts or call logs

3. How Our Tools Work

All NaijaTools features process data entirely on your device:

  • PDF Tools: All PDF processing (merge, split, compress, rotate) runs locally in the app. No file is transmitted over the internet.
  • Image Tools: Image compression, resizing, cropping, and format conversion happen on-device using your device's processing power.
  • Calculators: GPA, CGPA, JAMB, loan, and all other calculators run entirely client-side with no server communication.
  • QR Code Generator: QR codes are generated on-device. We do not store the content you encode.
  • Currency Converter: Exchange rates displayed are pre-loaded estimates. The app does not call any external API with your data.
  • Invoice Generator: Invoices are generated and saved locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.

4. Permissions We Request

The app requests the following Android permissions, all of which are used solely for on-device functionality:

  • INTERNET: Required to load web fonts and external resources for the app interface. No personal data is transmitted.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Required to allow you to open files from your device storage (PDFs, images) and save processed files back to your device.
  • CAMERA (optional): Required only if you choose to take a photo directly within the app for image processing tools.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check connectivity status for offline mode detection.

5. Third-Party Services

NaijaTools uses the following third-party libraries, all of which operate locally:

  • QRCode.js — Open-source QR code generation library. No data leaves your device.
  • Google Fonts (Inter) — Fonts loaded from Google's CDN on first use. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Material Symbols — Icon font loaded from Google's CDN. Same as above.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — Stylesheet loaded from CDN on first use. No personal data transmitted.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.), or crash reporting services that collect user data.

6. Data Storage

NaijaTools may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • App preferences (e.g. dark mode setting)
  • Cached web assets for offline functionality (via Service Worker)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data in your Android settings.

7. Children's Privacy

NaijaTools is suitable for all ages including students. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we collect no personal data from any user, this requirement is automatically satisfied.

8. Security

Since NaijaTools does not transmit or store your data on external servers, the risk to your data is minimal. All processing occurs within the secure sandbox of your Android device. We recommend keeping your Android OS updated for the best security posture.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. However, you may:

  • Clear all app data via Android Settings → Apps → NaijaTools → Clear Data
  • Uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored preferences
  • Contact us with any privacy questions or concerns
